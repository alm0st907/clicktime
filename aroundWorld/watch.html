Around the World!

Welcome to the Around the World webapp!

To use this app, first click on the permission button. Once you have given the application permission, the start/stop button will craete an entry in the table with your location (if found) and a time.

Upon refresh or exit of the page, data should be saved locally automatically, but there is a save button provided for peace of mind.

The clear data/table button will remove all entries on the table and remove all locally stored data.
